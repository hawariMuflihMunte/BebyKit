Background Color

You can apply this background color in almost all element, except outlined buttons and outlined button group. Implementing it is also easy, just directly call the class and ta-da! Your element has its own unique color now.

blue-bg 
<div class="blue-bg p-s">
    blue-bg
</div>

Text Color

Similar to Background Color. You can apply this only on texts.

light-text 
<div class="dark-bg light-text p-s">
    light-text
</div>
